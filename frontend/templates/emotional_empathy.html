Empathy means understanding how others feel.

Empathy is remembering that every human being carries an invisible struggle and deserves to find at least one heart willing to walk alongside them.

Here are five ways to practice empathy:

1. We should pay full attention to what someone is saying, without interrupting or judging, demonstrating genuine interest in their emotions and needs.
2. We must recognize and accept another person's emotions as valid, even if we don't agree with the situation or their choices.
3. We should think before we speak and consider whether our words might hurt someone.
4. We must understand that sometimes the best response is a hug or a gesture of support, rather than words.
5. A kind word or gesture can make someone's day much better.

Try smiling at someone today, it costs nothing, but it can mean everything.
